About

AZANIA MODELS PTY is a premier modeling agency and academy that specializes in recruiting, outsourcing, and training models. With a focus on nurturing talent and providing comprehensive training, we prepare our models for successful careers in the fashion and entertainment industries.

Experience & Achievements

Mthobisi Mkhulise (Co-Founder and agency director) has over 10 years of experience in the digital industry, with a strong background in graphic work and digital art. As a natural-born graphic specialist, Azania has leveraged their expertise to create visually stunning campaigns and training materials that have helped many aspiring models launch their careers.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to discover and cultivate exceptional modeling talent, providing them with the tools and opportunities to succeed in a competitive industry.

Vision: We envision a modeling industry where every model has the opportunity to shine, regardless of their background, and where creativity and professionalism are celebrated.

Values & Philosophy

At AZANIA MODELS PTY, we believe in:

  • Integrity: Conducting our business with honesty and transparency.
